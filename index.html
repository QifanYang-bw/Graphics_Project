User Instructions

1. You can drag the mouse in canvas to see the drone in all directions. The axes will rotate accordingly.
2. You can use 'WASD' to control the camera's location.
3. You can use 'IJKL' to move the look point.
4. You can speed up the animation by clicking the "Speed Up" button.
5. You can slow down the animation by clicking the "Slow Down" button.
6. You can pause the the animation by clicking the "Run / Stop" button or press 'p' on keyboard.
7. If you press "Space" on keyboard, there will be a wine glass 'breaking' into the canvas. Guess what reaction they will make.
8. If you double click on canvas, they will make the same reaction. Try it!
