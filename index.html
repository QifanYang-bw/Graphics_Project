User Instructions

- Use the control panel on the right side to adjust most of the settings.

Keyboard Instructions

- 'M': Change the material of the sphere.
- 'ZX': Increase/Decrease the shininess of the specific sphere material.

- 'WASD': Perspective Translate.
- 'IJKL': Perspective Rotation.
- 'Space': Causes a wine glass to appear in the field, with corresponding animations.

- 'P': Pause/Resume animation.
- 'H': Show/Hide the control panel.
